Resource Economics Society Marketing

I served as the Marketing Coordinator and Secretary for the Resource Economics Society, a student-run club that helps students in my deparment connect with each other socially while helping them prepare for their careers! I've since taken over as president of the club, but these are the marketing designs I created during that time with the use of Canva.


PWTC Design Projects

Here I've compiled my favorite design products from my techncial writing courses. I've included a magazine spread, more marketing materials, and the logo for this website, all of which I created in Adobe InDesign or Illustrator.

  • Two-Page Spread Magazine




    This is a two-page magazine spread created with Adobe InDesign. What I like most about this project is my attention to visual interest; the sunflower step numbers, the "stained glass" arrangement of images on the fourth page, the colors pulled from the first image, and the scalloped petal border of the title.
